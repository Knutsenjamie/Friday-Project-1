About Me

Hello, my name is Jamie Knutsen. If there's anything important to know about me, it's that I've got a huge passion for food. Not just any food, but making vegan food taste good. I've been a vegan chef for over 5 years, and have always been very passionate about it. I graduated from Clover Park Technical College back in 2018 with an AA in Culinary Arts. While I had a good run professionally as a chef (I became a kitchen manager fresh out of school), COVID hit and the restaurant industry took a pretty huge blow. I became unemployed for quite some time. During that up-in-the-air period; wondering what I could do with my skills, I created my own vegan food blog with my own recipes. I studied, learned some basic coding, and created my own wordpress site and opened my own LLC with all self-taught experience. It was definitely not easy! I even bought camera equiptment and studied legal background so that I knew what I was doing. Thus, my baby VitallyVeg.com was born! After taking care of my site for almost a year, and still havig no leads for the restuarant industry or income, I knew it was time to start anew. I thought to myself; why not expand on some coding skills to not only get a more secure source of income, but also better my own site and business? These are the reasons as to why I knew Epicodus would be the perfect fit. It's been a great journey thus far!
